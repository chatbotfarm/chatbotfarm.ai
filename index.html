FAQ

Do you replace my staff?

We observe your current business operation and map it onto CRM. Repetitive tasks are replaced with workflows, so staff can concentrate on billable operations and reduce overhead.

What happens when AI is unsure?

It escalates cleanly: the system sends a memo to your assigned team member with the conversation context and a clear request to clarify next steps.

Do you sit between me and my customers?

No. We build on tools in your name and your business owns the relationship, the records, and the workflows. We don’t sell your data and we don’t take a “platform tax” on your customer relationships.

Can this work with what I already use?

Usually, yes. On the demo call we identify what you want to keep, what should be connected, and what should be replaced with automation. The goal is fewer moving parts, not more.

Pricing philosophy

Transparent pricing: tools in your name + a clear service margin for engineering, maintenance, and tuning. No black-box platform lock-in.
