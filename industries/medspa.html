Common Inquiry Categories

The Digital Office routes each interaction based on intent and your rules.

  • New client questions (services, pricing ranges, availability)
  • Appointment booking and rescheduling
  • Pre-visit intake and basic eligibility questions
  • Post-visit follow-ups and aftercare reminders
  • Membership / package questions
  • Reviews and retention follow-ups
  • Urgent concerns (escalation path to staff)

Structured Intake Fields

Instead of unstructured back-and-forth, the system captures consistent data your staff can act on.

  • Client name and contact details
  • Preferred service(s)
  • Preferred date/time windows
  • New vs returning client
  • Key notes (skin goals, concerns, time sensitivity)
  • Consent to receive reminders and follow-ups (when applicable)

If your clinic uses intake forms, the system can send a link by text and attach responses to the client record.

Workflows That Reduce Admin Load

Once the intake data is captured, automation handles the repetitive steps.

  • Send booking links and confirmation messages
  • Reminders to reduce no-shows
  • Pre-visit instructions
  • Aftercare reminders and check-ins
  • Review requests (timed to your preference)
  • Reactivation campaigns for inactive clients

When Human Judgment Is Needed

The goal is not to replace your staff—it’s to replace repetitive tasks with workflows. Your team stays focused on billable operations and client care.

When the AI is unsure, the system escalates cleanly:

  • A memo is sent to the assigned team member
  • The conversation context is included
  • A clear request explains what needs a decision

This prevents the AI from over-promising and keeps your clinic in control.
