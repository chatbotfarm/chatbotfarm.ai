Construction Digital Office

You run crews. We code. Chatbot Farm keeps every call, request, and update organized so your team can stay on the jobsite.

Call Categories

  • New project inquiries and bids
  • Active jobsite updates and scheduling
  • Service requests and warranty calls
  • Vendor coordination and deliveries

Intake Fields

  • Jobsite address, project type, and timeline
  • Caller name, company, and callback details
  • Scope notes, photos, and urgency level
  • Preferred crew, trade, or service line

Routing

Requests are routed to the right estimator, superintendent, or service coordinator based on trade, region, and priority. The digital office keeps every thread visible in one place.

Follow-Ups

Automated follow-ups confirm schedules, capture approvals, and close the loop with customers and crews without extra admin work.

When AI Is Unsure

If the AI cannot confidently answer, it creates an escalation memo with the full call summary and routes it to your team for a same-day response.
