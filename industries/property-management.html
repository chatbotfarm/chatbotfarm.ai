Common Call & Message Categories

The Digital Office listens first, then routes each interaction based on intent.

  • Rental availability and general property inquiries
  • Showing requests and scheduling
  • Maintenance and repair requests
  • Application status and leasing questions
  • Vendor coordination and follow-ups
  • Urgent issues and after-hours escalation

Structured Intake Fields

Instead of free-form messages, the system collects consistent, usable data.

  • Property address or unit number
  • Type of request (showing, maintenance, application, general question)
  • Tenant or prospect name and contact details
  • Description of issue or request
  • Photos or documents (when applicable)
  • Urgency level

Routing & Workflows

Once information is captured, workflows take over automatically.

  • Showing requests are routed to scheduling workflows
  • Maintenance tickets are assigned to the correct vendor or internal team
  • Tenants receive confirmation and status updates automatically
  • Property managers see a clean pipeline of open items
  • Follow-ups and reminders trigger without manual intervention

When Human Judgment Is Needed

The goal is not to replace your staff—it’s to remove repetitive work.

When the AI is unsure, the system escalates cleanly:

  • A memo is sent to the assigned team member
  • The full conversation context is included
  • A clear request explains what needs a decision

Your team stays in control, without being interrupted by routine questions.
